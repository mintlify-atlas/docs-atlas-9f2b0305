Baklog is a **local-first** game backlog manager that connects to twelve storefronts and brings everything you own into one unified, deduplicated library. Sign in to each store once, and Baklog auto-fetches your games, enriches them with HLTB hours, review scores, and deal prices — all running on your own machine, with no cloud backend holding your data.

## What Baklog does

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your stores">
    Open the **Connections** tab and click **Connect** for each store. A Chrome/Edge window opens for cookie or OAuth sign-in. Credentials are encrypted with your OS keyring — they never leave your machine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fetch your libraries">
    After connecting, Baklog **auto-fetches** each store and streams the results live into the fetcher log. Every store lights up as its library lands.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your backlog">
    Browse the **Dashboard** for analytics, the **Library** for your deduped cross-store table, and the **Wishlist** deal radar for ownership-aware pricing. Use the Picks panel to decide what to play next.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay current">
    Auto-refresh quietly updates one stale store every ~30 minutes while the app is open. The optional paid tier adds background scheduled refresh even when the app is closed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
